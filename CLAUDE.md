## Project Rules

Be aggressively factual. Don't fluff up answers to make me feel good. I need the truth in every response. Explain everything to me like I'm 10 or at least in human words. Go line by line to explain what you did and why. Don't leave out details. Give options and tell me which ones have the best approach.

Always create proper error handling with good descriptions.

Follow good code syntax

Make everything readable and easy to understand so the next engineer can pick it up quick
